WINES AND COCKTAILS

Cabernet Sauvignon

Red wine with rich flavors of blackcurrant, blackberry.

Chardonnay

A versatile white wine with flavors ranging from crisp apple and citrus to buttery notes and vanilla.

Sauvignon Blanc

A refreshing white wine with vibrant acidity and flavors of citrus, tropical fruits, and grassy notes.

Margarita

A classic cocktail made with tequila, lime juice, and orange liqueur (such as triple sec or Cointreau).

Mojito

A refreshing cocktail made with white rum, lime juice, simple syrup, fresh mint leaves, and club soda.

Manhattan

A classic cocktail made with whiskey (typically rye), sweet vermouth, and bitters. It is stirred with ice and strained into a cocktail glass, often garnished with a cherry.

Merlot

A medium-bodied red wine with flavors of black cherry, plum, and chocolate.

New York Cheesecake

Cheesecake made with cream cheese, eggs, sugar, and a graham cracker crust.

Crème Brûlée

A classic French dessert consisting of a smooth and creamy custard topped with a caramelized sugar crust.

Key Lime Pie

A refreshing and tangy dessert made with a sweet and tart filling primarily composed of key lime juice and condensed milk, served in a buttery graham cracker crust.

Brownie Sundae

A delightful combination of warm, fudgy brownies topped with a scoop (or multiple scoops) of ice cream, drizzled with chocolate sauce, and garnished with whipped cream, nuts, and a cherry.
